#+title: monome for 72-edo on 12-edo keyboard scheme
* A 13x13 grid.
* 12 columns correspond to the 12 keyboard notes.
  The five black keys flash.
* The 13th column is a global offset.
* Each column has a range of [-6,6] 72-edo step offsets.
  i.e. a [-1,1] halfstep range of offset values,
  quantized to 72-edo.
