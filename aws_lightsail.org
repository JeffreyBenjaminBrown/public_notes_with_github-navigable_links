#+title: AWS Lightsail
A higher-level interface to [[https://github.com/JeffreyBenjaminBrown/public_notes_with_github-navigable_links/blob/master/aws_virtual_machine_via_ec2.org][AWS EC2]].
Hides many configuration details, e.g. security.
Has been accused of burying errors in logs and being harder to manipulate outside of its default parameters.
